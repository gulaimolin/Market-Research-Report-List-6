<p><ul><li>Single Stage Module</li><li>Multistage Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermoelectric module market is primarily categorized into single stage and multistage modules. Single stage modules consist of one thermoelectric pair, ideal for compact cooling or heating applications with moderate temperature differentials. In contrast, multistage modules feature multiple thermoelectric pairs stacked together, enabling more significant temperature differences and enhanced cooling capabilities. These modules are suitable for applications requiring extreme temperature control, such as in scientific instruments and specialized electronic devices, providing efficient thermal management across various industries.</p></p>
