<p><p>The bioremediation technology market encompasses various approaches to mitigate environmental pollution. Bioreactors facilitate controlled biological processes to treat contaminants. Biostimulation enhances native microbial activity using nutrients, while bioaugmentation introduces specialized microbes to degrade pollutants. Fungal remediation utilizes fungi for decomposition and pollutant absorption. Phytoremediation integrates plants to uptake and detoxify harmful substances from soil or water. Land-based treatments utilize soil microbes or amendments to address contamination, improving ecosystem health and promoting sustainable environmental practices.</p></p>
