<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) coaters market consists of three main types: Thermal Evaporation, Sputter Deposition, and Arc Vapor Deposition. Thermal Evaporation involves heating materials in a vacuum to create vapor, which condenses onto the substrate. Sputter Deposition uses energetic ions to eject material from a target, depositing it onto the surface. Arc Vapor Deposition generates a plasma arc to vaporize metal, creating coatings with high adhesion and density. Each method serves various industries, enhancing product durability and performance.</p></p>
