<p><p>A laminated bus bar is a type of bus bar that is made by stacking layers of copper and insulating materials, such as Mylar or Kapton, and then laminating them together under high pressure and temperature. This results in a compact, low-inductance, and high-power density bus bar that is used in a wide range of industries including power electronics, automotive, aerospace, and telecommunications.</p><p>The laminated bus bar market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
