<p><p>The eco-friendly plastic bags market is primarily categorized into three types: Polylactic Acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), and Starch Blends. PLA is derived from renewable resources like corn and is biodegradable, making it a popular choice for sustainable packaging. PHA, produced by microbial fermentation, is also biodegradable and compostable, offering a versatile alternative to traditional plastics. Starch Blends combine starch with other polymers, providing a biodegradable option that can be cost-effective while minimizing environmental impact. Each type addresses sustainability concerns while serving various market needs.</p></p>
