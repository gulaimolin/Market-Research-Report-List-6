<p><p>The 3D modeling market comprises various types, primarily NURBS and polygon mesh. NURBS (Non-Uniform Rational B-Splines) modeling excels in creating smooth and complex curves, making it ideal for automotive and product design due to its precision and flexibility. In contrast, polygon mesh modeling constructs objects using vertices, edges, and faces, which is widely used in gaming and animation for its versatility and efficiency in rendering real-time graphics. Both methods serve distinct industry needs, enhancing creative possibilities.</p></p>
