<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G base station antennas market is primarily divided into passive and active types. Passive antennas simply receive and transmit signals without processing, relying on external equipment for amplification and signal management. In contrast, active antennas integrate signal processing within the antenna itself, enhancing performance and enabling features like beamforming. Active antennas generally offer improved efficiency, reduced space requirements, and better coverage, making them increasingly popular for modern networks. Each type plays a crucial role in the deployment of 5G technology.</p></p>
