<p><strong>The Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP)</li><li>Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP)</li><li>Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) machines market comprises three main types: Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) delivers a steady airflow to maintain open airways in patients with sleep apnea. Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) provides two pressure settings for inhalation and exhalation, accommodating users with varying respiratory needs. Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV) adjusts airflow based on the patient's breathing patterns, offering personalized support for complex sleep apnea. Collectively, these devices enhance respiratory therapy and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
