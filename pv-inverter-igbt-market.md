<p><ul><li>IGBT Modules</li><li>IGBT Discretes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV inverter IGBT market is primarily segmented into IGBT modules and IGBT discretes. IGBT modules integrate multiple IGBT devices within a single package, offering high efficiency and compact design, ideal for large-scale solar applications. In contrast, IGBT discretes consist of individual transistor components, allowing for flexible design and customization in smaller or specialized applications. Both types play a crucial role in converting direct current from solar panels to usable alternating current, supporting the growth of solar energy systems.</p></p>
