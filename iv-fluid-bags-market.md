<p><p>The IV fluid bags market comprises three main types: flex plastic intravenous fluid bags, semi-rigid intravenous fluid bags, and glass bottles. Flex plastic bags are lightweight and flexible, allowing for easy handling and storage, while semi-rigid bags provide more stability and protection against contamination. Glass bottles, though less commonly used today, offer excellent chemical compatibility and stability for certain medications but are heavier and more prone to breakage. Each type serves specific needs in medical settings, impacting usability and safety.</p></p>
