<p><p>The electronic load market comprises three primary types: benchtop, system, and module. System electronic loads are integrated solutions designed for high-power applications, often used in testing large systems like power supplies and batteries. Module electronic loads are modular, scalable units that can be combined to accommodate various testing requirements, offering flexibility for diverse applications in research and development.</p></p>
