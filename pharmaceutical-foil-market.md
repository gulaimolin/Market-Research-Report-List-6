<p><ul><li>Cold Forming Aluminum Foil</li><li>Tropical Aluminum Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical foil market primarily consists of two types: cold forming aluminum foil and tropical aluminum foil. Cold forming aluminum foil is used for forming blisters that provide superior moisture, light, and oxygen barrier properties, ensuring the protection of sensitive medications. Tropical aluminum foil, on the other hand, is designed for high-humidity environments, offering enhanced resistance against moisture. Both types are crucial for maintaining the integrity and efficacy of pharmaceutical products throughout their shelf life.</p></p>
