CSP/BGA board-level underfills are designed for chip-scale packages and ball grid arrays, offering mechanical stability and thermal conductivity. Each type plays a crucial role in mitigating thermal and mechanical stress in electronic devices, ensuring longevity and efficiency.</p></p>
