<p><p>The Pressure Reducing Sets market comprises two main types: simplex and duplex pressure reducing sets. Simplex pressure reducing sets consist of a single unit that manages pressure for various applications, ideal for straightforward systems where redundancy isn't necessary. Conversely, duplex pressure reducing sets feature two parallel units, ensuring continuous operation even during maintenance or failure of one unit. This design is favored in critical applications where reliability and uninterrupted supply are paramount, enhancing overall system resilience and efficiency.</p></p>
