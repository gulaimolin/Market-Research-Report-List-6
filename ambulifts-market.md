<p><strong>The Ambulifts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SideBull</li><li>FrontBull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ambulifts come in two main market types - SideBull and FrontBull. SideBull ambulifts are designed to lift passengers from the side of the aircraft, making them ideal for narrow-bodied planes with low doors. On the other hand, FrontBull ambulifts lift passengers directly from the front of the aircraft, making them suitable for wide-bodied planes with high doors. Both types of ambulifts serve the same purpose of providing safe and efficient boarding and disembarkation for passengers with reduced mobility.</p></p>
