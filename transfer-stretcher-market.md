<p><p>The transfer stretcher market consists of three main types: electric stretchers, hydraulic stretchers, and manual transfer stretchers. Electric stretchers feature motorized mechanisms for height adjustment and positioning, enhancing convenience and efficiency in patient transport. Hydraulic stretchers rely on hydraulic systems for flexibility and ease of use, making them suitable for varying terrain and patient needs. Manual transfer stretchers require physical effort for adjustment and movement, offering a cost-effective solution for facilities with budget constraints. Each type serves specific operational requirements in healthcare settings.</p></p>
