<p><p>Fresh meat packaging films are categorized based on material types, each offering unique properties. Polyethylene (PE) is flexible and moisture-resistant, while Polypropylene (PP) provides good clarity and strength. Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) enhances barrier properties and printability. Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) offers excellent oxygen barrier, ideal for preserving freshness. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is cost-effective but less popular due to environmental concerns. Polyamide (PA) provides strong barrier properties and puncture resistance. Other materials may include biodegradable options or multi-layer composites for enhanced performance.</p></p>
