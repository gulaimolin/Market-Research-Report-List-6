<p><p>The Forklift Seating System market comprises two main types: Mechanical Suspension Seats and Air Suspension Seats. Mechanical Suspension Seats utilize springs and dampers to absorb shock and vibrations, providing basic comfort and support. In contrast, Air Suspension Seats use an air-filled bladder system to adjust firmness based on the operator's weight, offering enhanced cushioning and customized comfort over uneven surfaces. Both types aim to improve operator safety, reduce fatigue, and enhance productivity during forklift operation in various industries.</p></p>
