<p><p>Standard reed switches, typically rated for 3W and 20W applications, are essential components in various electronic circuits. The market features two primary types: Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC) switches. Normally Open switches remain open until a magnetic field is applied, allowing current to flow, ideal for triggering devices. In contrast, Normally Closed switches maintain a closed circuit until interrupted by a magnetic field, suitable for safety applications. These switches cater to diverse industries, including automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
