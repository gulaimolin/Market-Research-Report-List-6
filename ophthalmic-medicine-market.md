<p><p>The ophthalmic medicine market encompasses various types of formulations designed to treat eye conditions. Eye drops are liquid solutions applied directly to the eye for quick absorption. Eye ointments provide a thicker, longer-lasting application, ideal for overnight use. Gelling agents enhance the viscosity of these formulations, improving retention time on the ocular surface. Tablets offer oral alternatives for systemic treatment, while injections deliver medications directly into the eye, addressing more severe conditions. Together, these options cater to diverse therapeutic needs.</p></p>
