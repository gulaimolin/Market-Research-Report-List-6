<p><p>The brake blocks market can be categorized into three main types: disc brakes, drum brakes, and other brakes. Disc brakes use calipers to squeeze brake pads against a disc rotor to slow down or stop the vehicle. Drum brakes work by applying pressure to brake shoes against the inside of a drum to create friction and stop the vehicle. The "other" category includes unique brake systems such as regenerative braking and electromagnetic brakes, which are gaining popularity in the market for their efficiency and precision.</p></p>
