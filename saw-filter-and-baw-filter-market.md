<p><p>SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) filters and BAW (Bulk Acoustic Wave) filters are essential components in modern communication systems, used to control signal frequencies and ensure quality in wireless communication. SAW filters utilize surface acoustic waves traveling along the surface of a material, primarily in applications like mobile phones and television receivers. In contrast, BAW filters operate using bulk acoustic waves within a piezoelectric material, offering advantages such as size reduction and improved performance at higher frequencies, making them suitable for 5G applications and other high-frequency operations.</p><p>The market for SAW and BAW filters is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-frequency and miniaturized devices, as well as the proliferation of IoT devices and growing wireless communication sectors.
