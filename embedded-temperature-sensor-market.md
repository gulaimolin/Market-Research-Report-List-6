<p><ul><li>Non-Contact</li><li>Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded temperature sensor market comprises two primary types: non-contact and contact sensors. Non-contact sensors measure temperature from a distance using infrared technology, making them ideal for applications where the sensor cannot touch the object, such as in electrical equipment or moving machinery. In contrast, contact sensors require direct contact with the object to measure temperature accurately, commonly used in industrial processes, HVAC systems, and food safety applications. Both types cater to specific needs, enhancing temperature monitoring across various industries.</p></p>
