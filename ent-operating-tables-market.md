<p><p>The ENT operating tables market consists of three main types: electric, hydraulic, and manual operating tables. Electric operating tables offer precise positioning and adjustments with minimal effort, enhancing efficiency during surgical procedures. Hydraulic tables utilize a fluid-based system for height and position adjustments, providing stability and ease of use. Manual operating tables require physical effort for adjustments but are typically more cost-effective and simpler in design. Each type caters to different budgetary and operational needs within the ENT surgical environment.</p></p>
