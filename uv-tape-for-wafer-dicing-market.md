<p><ul><li>Single-sided</li><li>Double-sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV tape for wafer dicing is designed to secure semiconductor wafers during the dicing process. Single-sided UV tape features adhesive on one side, providing a strong bond to the wafer while allowing easy release after dicing. In contrast, double-sided UV tape has adhesive on both sides, enabling it to stick to both the wafer and the cutting surface, enhancing stability and alignment. Both types are crucial for minimizing damage and ensuring precision in the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
