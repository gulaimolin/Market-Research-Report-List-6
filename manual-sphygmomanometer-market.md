<p><strong>The Manual Sphygmomanometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aneroid Sphygmomanometer</li><li>Mercury Sphygmomanometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual sphygmomanometer market includes two primary types: aneroid and mercury sphygmomanometers. Aneroid sphygmomanometers utilize a mechanical dial to measure blood pressure, offering portability and ease of use, making them popular in clinics and home settings. Mercury sphygmomanometers, considered the gold standard for accuracy, use a column of mercury to gauge pressure but face regulatory challenges due to mercury toxicity. Both types serve essential roles in accurate blood pressure monitoring, particularly in clinical environments.</p></p>
