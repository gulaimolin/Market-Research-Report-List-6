<p><p>The semiconductor chip testing tool market encompasses various types designed for different testing needs. Nondestructive testing tools ensure chip integrity without damaging them. External inspection devices visually assess chip surfaces for defects. Electrical testing tools measure the electrical characteristics of chips, ensuring they meet performance standards. Voltage and current testing tools evaluate the chip's power handling capabilities. Fault testing tools identify specific malfunctioning areas within chips, ensuring reliable performance in electronic applications, thus fostering quality assurance in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
