<p><p>The digital electronic thermometer market features various types, prominently the forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead thermometers use infrared technology to quickly measure temperature by scanning the forehead, making them non-intrusive and suitable for all ages. Ear thermometers, on the other hand, utilize infrared sensors to measure temperature inside the ear canal, providing accurate readings, particularly in children. Both types are favored for their speed and ease of use, driving demand in healthcare and home settings.</p></p>
