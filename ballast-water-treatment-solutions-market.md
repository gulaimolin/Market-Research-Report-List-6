<p><p>The Ballast Water Treatment Solutions Market encompasses two main types: chemical methods and physical methods. Chemical methods involve the use of biocides or other substances to eliminate invasive species from ballast water, offering a direct approach to treatment. In contrast, physical methods utilize processes like filtration, UV radiation, or thermal treatments to physically remove or inactivate organisms without chemicals. Both approaches aim to comply with international regulations, ensuring environmental protection and the prevention of marine biodiversity loss.</p></p>
