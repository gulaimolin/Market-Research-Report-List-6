<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite resins market is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastic and thermosetting resins. Thermoplastic resins can be re-melted and reshaped multiple times, making them versatile for applications requiring flexibility and impact resistance. They are often used in automotive and aerospace industries. In contrast, thermosetting resins undergo a curing process that hardens them permanently. This type offers excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for electrical and industrial applications. Both types play crucial roles in various manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
