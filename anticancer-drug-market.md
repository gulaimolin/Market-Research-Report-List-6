<p><p>The anticancer drug market can be broadly classified into four main types: targeted drugs, cytotoxic drugs, hormonal drugs, and others. Targeted drugs specifically target cancer cells while sparing healthy cells, leading to fewer side effects. Cytotoxic drugs work by directly killing cancer cells. Hormonal drugs disrupt the hormonal balance in the body to slow down or stop the growth of hormone-sensitive cancers. Other types of drugs in the market include immunotherapy and angiogenesis inhibitors which work to enhance the body's immune response against cancer cells and block blood vessel formation in tumors, respectively.</p></p>
