<p><p>The crop cleaning equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: pre-cleaning and fine cleaning. Pre-cleaning equipment is designed to remove larger contaminants such as leaves, stems, and rocks before the main cleaning process. Fine cleaning equipment, on the other hand, targets smaller impurities, including dust and smaller particles, ensuring a higher quality grain output. Both types are essential for enhancing the efficiency of grain processing and ensuring the delivery of cleaner, higher-quality agricultural products.</p></p>
