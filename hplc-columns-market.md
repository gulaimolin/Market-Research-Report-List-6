<p><strong>The HPLC Columns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversed-Phase</li><li>Normal-Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HPLC columns market can be divided into two main types: reversed-phase and normal-phase. Reversed-phase columns feature non-polar stationary phases and are widely used for separating polar compounds, making them suitable for biological samples and pharmaceuticals. Conversely, normal-phase columns utilize polar stationary phases, ideal for separating non-polar compounds and often used in applications like natural product isolation. Each type offers distinct advantages based on the chemical properties of the analytes, catering to various analytical needs in different industries.</p></p>
