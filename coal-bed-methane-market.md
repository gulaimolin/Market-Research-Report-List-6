<p><p>The coal bed methane market involves extracting natural gas from coal seams using advanced techniques like horizontal drilling and hydraulic fracturing. Horizontal drilling allows for tapping into extensive coal deposits, enhancing gas flow rates. Hydraulic fracturing, or fracking, involves injecting high-pressure fluids into the coal beds to create fractures, improving gas recovery. Proppants, materials used to keep these fractures open, play a crucial role in maximizing gas extraction efficiency, thus driving the overall growth of the coal bed methane sector.</p></p>
