<p><p>Particle board adhesives market can be categorized into three types based on the adhesive used: solvent-based, water-based, and solventless adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain volatile organic compounds which can be harmful to the environment and human health. Water-based adhesives are more eco-friendly and have lower levels of volatile organic compounds. Solventless adhesives are a safer option as they do not contain any harmful solvents. Each type of adhesive offers different benefits and drawbacks, catering to a variety of consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
