<p><p>The cell filtration devices market comprises several types tailored for specific applications. Bench scale vacuum-driven devices use suction to filter cells in small batches, ideal for lab experiments. Bench scale pressure-driven devices utilize applied pressure for efficient filtration in similar settings. Lab scale vacuum-driven devices are designed for slightly larger volumes with enhanced control. Conversely, lab scale pump-driven devices employ mechanical pumps to achieve consistent flow rates, suitable for extensive analysis in cell culture and research applications.</p></p>
