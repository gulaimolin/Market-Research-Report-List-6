<p><p>The Intelligent Virtual Assistants (IVA) market can be classified into two main types: Rule-Based and Conversational AI-Based assistants. Rule-Based assistants operate on pre-defined scripts and decision trees, providing responses based on specific keywords or commands, making them suitable for straightforward tasks. Conversely, Conversational AI-Based assistants utilize advanced natural language processing and machine learning to understand and engage in more complex, human-like interactions. This enables them to manage intricate queries and learn over time, enhancing user experience significantly.</p></p>
