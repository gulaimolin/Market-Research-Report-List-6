On the other hand, hemodialysis dry powders are solid formulations that can be reconstituted into liquid solutions for dialysis treatment. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimal electrolyte balance and facilitating effective waste removal during hemodialysis sessions for patients with kidney failure.</p></p>
