<p><p>The preclinical tomography system market encompasses various imaging modalities that facilitate advanced research in small animal studies. CT (computed tomography) offers high-resolution anatomical imaging, while PET (positron emission tomography) provides functional imaging by tracking metabolic processes. SPECT (single photon emission computed tomography) delivers dynamic imaging to assess physiological functions. Composite systems integrate two or more modalities, enhancing the ability to correlate anatomical and functional data, ultimately improving the depth of analysis and efficacy in preclinical research applications.</p></p>
