<p><p>Hearing implants market comprises of various types such as Cochlear implants, Mid-Ear Implants, Bone Anchored Hearing Aids (BAHA), and Auditory Brainstem Implant (ABI). Cochlear implants are designed for individuals with severe to profound hearing loss by stimulating the auditory nerve directly. Mid-Ear Implants are placed in the middle ear to bypass problems in the outer or middle ear. BAHA devices transmit sound vibrations through bone to the inner ear. ABI is used for patients with significant damage to the cochlea or auditory nerve.</p></p>
