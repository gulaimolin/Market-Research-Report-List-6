<p><p>Liquid macrofiltration filters are used to remove solid particles and impurities from liquids in various industries. Some common types of macrofiltration filters include granular micro filters, leaf tubular and press filters, bag filters, belt filter presses, and other specialty filters. Granular micro filters use a bed of granular material to trap particles, while leaf tubular and press filters utilize a series of discs or tubes to separate solids. Bag filters are used to remove larger particles, while belt filter presses use a continuous belt to squeeze liquid out of a slurry. Other types of macrofiltration filters include drum filters, disc filters, and screen filters.</p></p>
