<p><strong>The Antidegradants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Antiozonants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antidegradants market consists primarily of two types: antioxidants and antiozonants. Antioxidants are additives that prevent oxidation, enhancing the longevity and performance of rubber and plastics by stabilizing the materials against deterioration caused by heat, light, and environmental factors. Antiozonants, on the other hand, specifically protect against ozone degradation, which can cause cracking and deterioration in rubber products. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, to maintain the integrity and reliability of materials.</p></p>
