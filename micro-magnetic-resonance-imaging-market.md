<p><strong>The Micro Magnetic Resonance Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open MRI</li><li>Closed MRI</li><li>Standard Bore MRI</li><li>Wide-Bore MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro Magnetic Resonance Imaging (μMRI) encompasses various types based on design and application. Open MRI features a wide-opening structure, reducing patient anxiety and accommodating larger sizes but may offer lower resolution. Closed MRI, with a confined space, provides high-resolution imaging but can be claustrophobic. Standard Bore MRI features a typical tunnel diameter, balancing comfort and image quality. Wide-Bore MRI caters to larger patients while still ensuring good image quality, making it a versatile choice in clinical settings.</p></p>
