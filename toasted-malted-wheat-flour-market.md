<p><p>Toasted Malted Wheat Flour is available in two main market types: Diastatic and Non-Diastatic. Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour contains active enzymes that break down starches, aiding in fermentation and dough development. This type is commonly used in bread making for increased rise and flavor development. Non-Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour, on the other hand, does not contain active enzymes and is often used for its rich, toasted flavor in baked goods where extra fermentation is not desired.</p></p>
