<p><p>The hybrid adapters market can be categorized into two main types: single-mode and multi-mode. Single-mode hybrid adapters are designed for long-distance data transmission, utilizing a single light wavelength, which enhances speed and reduces signal loss over extended ranges. In contrast, multi-mode hybrid adapters facilitate shorter-distance communication by using multiple wavelengths, allowing for higher data rates over limited distances. Both types serve crucial roles in telecommunications and networking, ensuring efficient connectivity across diverse applications and environments.</p></p>
