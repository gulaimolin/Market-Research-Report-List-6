<p><strong>The Heavy Duty Safety Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fusible Disconnect</li><li>Non-fusible Disconnect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy-duty safety switch market is primarily divided into two types: fusible disconnects and non-fusible disconnects. Fusible disconnects include fuses that protect electrical circuits from overloads and short circuits, ensuring safe operation in industrial environments. They allow for easy replacement of fuses when necessary. In contrast, non-fusible disconnects lack fuses and are designed primarily to provide a means of disconnecting power safely without overcurrent protection. Both types are essential for ensuring safe electrical isolation in various industrial applications.</p></p>
