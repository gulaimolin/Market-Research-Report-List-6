<p><p>The vehicle counter market includes various types tailored for traffic monitoring. Inductive loop vehicle counters use electromagnetic sensors embedded in the road to detect vehicles. Pressure hose counters measure the weight of vehicles on a hose placed across the roadway. Infrared vehicle counters utilize light beams to count passing vehicles. Portable vehicle counters offer flexibility for temporary setups, while others encompass technology like GPS or video analytics. Each type serves specific needs in traffic management and data collection.</p></p>
