<p><p>The bioadhesives market is categorized into plant-based and animal-based types. Plant-based bioadhesives utilize natural polymers derived from sources like starch, cellulose, and lignin, providing sustainable alternatives in packaging and construction. In contrast, animal-based bioadhesives are derived from proteins, such as collagen and gelatin, often used in medical applications and woodworking. Both types emphasize environmentally friendly properties, offering non-toxic, biodegradable options that cater to the increasing demand for sustainable materials across various industries.</p></p>
