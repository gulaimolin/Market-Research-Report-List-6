<p><p>The automatic hand dryer market primarily features two types: hot air dryers and jet air dryers. Hot air dryers utilize a warm airflow to evaporate moisture from hands, providing a gentle drying experience but often taking longer to achieve full dryness. Jet air dryers, on the other hand, use high-velocity air streams to forcefully remove water quickly, resulting in faster drying times. Each type caters to different user preferences and settings, influencing their adoption in public restrooms and commercial spaces.</p></p>
