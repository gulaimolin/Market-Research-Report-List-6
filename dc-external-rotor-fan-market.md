<p><p>The DC external rotor fan market comprises three main types: axial flow fans, centrifugal fans, and other specialized fans. Axial flow fans move air parallel to the fan axis, making them ideal for high airflow and low resistance applications. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, draw air in at right angles and discharge it at increased pressure, suitable for ducted systems. Other market types may include mixed-flow fans or specialized designs, catering to specific needs in industrial, commercial, and residential settings.</p></p>
