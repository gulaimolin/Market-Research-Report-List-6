<p><p>Variable Conductance Heat Pipes (VCHPs) are specialized thermal management devices used in various industries. The "Wicked with Cold Reservoir" type employs a capillary wick structure to facilitate fluid movement, allowing efficient heat transfer from a hot source to a cooler area. Conversely, "Wickless with Hot Reservoir" designs eliminate the wick, utilizing pressure differences for fluid movement, which can enhance performance in certain applications. Both types optimize thermal regulation, catering to diverse operational needs in electronics and aerospace sectors.</p></p>
