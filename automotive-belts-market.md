Timing belts, on the other hand, synchronize the rotation of the crankshaft and camshaft, ensuring precise timing for engine performance. Both types are essential for vehicle functionality, contributing to efficiency, reliability, and overall engine health. Their demand fluctuates based on automotive production and maintenance needs.</p></p>
