<p><p>Vacuum Insulating Glass (VIG) is a high-performance glazing solution that employs a vacuum seal between two glass panes to significantly reduce heat transfer. The market for VIG is segmented into tempered and non-tempered types. Tempered VIG undergoes a thermal treatment process, enhancing its strength and thermal resistance, making it suitable for high-stress applications. Non-tempered VIG, while still effective in insulation, has lower structural integrity and is typically used in less demanding environments. Both types aim to improve energy efficiency in buildings.</p></p>
