<p><p>The glaucoma surgical devices market encompasses several key types aimed at managing intraocular pressure. Glaucoma drainage devices are implanted to facilitate the outflow of aqueous humor, reducing pressure. Glaucoma laser devices utilize focused light to improve drainage pathways or create openings for fluid outflow. Additionally, implants and stents are designed to maintain clear channels for aqueous humor, minimizing the need for medication. Collectively, these innovations offer effective treatment options for patients with varying degrees of glaucoma severity.</p></p>
