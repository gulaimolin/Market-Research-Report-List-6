<p><ul><li>Synchronous Motors</li><li>Asynchronous Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low horsepower AC motors market can be categorized into two main types: synchronous motors and asynchronous motors. Synchronous motors operate at a fixed speed determined by the frequency of the power source, making them suitable for applications requiring precise control. Asynchronous motors, on the other hand, operate at varying speeds based on load demand, making them ideal for general-purpose use. Both types of motors have their own advantages and are commonly used in various industries for low horsepower applications.</p></p>
