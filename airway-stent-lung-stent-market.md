<p><p>The airway and lung stent market primarily consists of three types: metal stents, silicone stents, and hybrid stents. Metal stents, often made from stainless steel or nitinol, provide strong structural support and are used for long-term airway dilation. Silicone stents are flexible and biocompatible, ideal for short-term use and minimizing irritation. Hybrid stents combine metal and silicone materials, offering a balance of durability and flexibility, catering to varied clinical needs and improving patient outcomes in airway management.</p></p>
