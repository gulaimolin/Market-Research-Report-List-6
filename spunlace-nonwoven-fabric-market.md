<p><strong>The Spunlace Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Lapped Spunlace Fabric</li><li>Cross Lapped Spunlace Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spunlace nonwoven fabric market consists of two main types: parallel-lapped and cross-lapped spunlace fabric. Parallel-lapped spunlace fabric features fibers laid in parallel, resulting in a smooth finish and consistent strength, ideal for applications requiring uniformity, such as hygiene products. In contrast, cross-lapped spunlace fabric is produced by interweaving fibers at angles, enhancing bulkiness and absorbency, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications like wipes and industrial uses. Both types offer unique properties catering to different market needs.</p></p>
