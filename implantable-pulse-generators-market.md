<p><p>Implantable Pulse Generators are devices used to deliver electrical pulses for various medical conditions. Single lumen IPGs are equipped with one catheter for pulse delivery, suitable for simpler procedures. Double lumen IPGs have two catheters to deliver different types of pulses simultaneously or provide backup options in case of catheter failure. Both types are used in a variety of medical applications, including pain management, neurostimulation, and cardiac pacing. The choice between single and double lumen IPGs depends on the specific medical needs of the patient.</p></p>
