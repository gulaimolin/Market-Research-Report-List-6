<p><p>The photovoltaic tracking bracket market is categorized into two main types: two-row component tracking and single-row component tracking. Two-row tracking systems utilize a design that allows multiple rows of solar panels to follow the sun’s movement, enhancing energy capture and efficiency. In contrast, single-row tracking systems feature a more compact design with fewer rows, making them suitable for smaller installations with lower energy demands. Both types aim to optimize solar energy production while accommodating different site conditions and requirements.</p></p>
