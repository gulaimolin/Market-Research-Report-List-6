<p><p>The disposable self-injection device market comprises three main types: pen injectors, autoinjectors, and wearable injectors. Pen injectors are user-friendly devices designed for self-administration of medications and often feature a dial mechanism for dosage adjustment. Autoinjectors provide a pre-measured dose and are typically activated with a simple button press, ensuring ease of use. Wearable injectors, designed for continuous or chronic medication delivery, attach to the skin and are ideal for longer-term therapy, offering convenience and discreetness for users.</p></p>
