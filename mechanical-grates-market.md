<p><strong>The Mechanical Grates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scroll Type</li><li>Reciprocating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical grates market consists primarily of two types: scroll type and reciprocating type. Scroll type grates feature a continuous, spiral motion that efficiently moves materials while providing consistent screening and separation. In contrast, reciprocating type grates employ a back-and-forth motion, allowing for effective material handling and optimized flow control. Both types serve various applications in solid waste management and industrial processes, enhancing operational efficiency and productivity in handling bulk materials and improving waste processing systems.</p></p>
