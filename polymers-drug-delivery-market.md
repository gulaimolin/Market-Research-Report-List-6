<p><p>Polymers drug delivery market types include micelles, which are nanoscale particles that can encapsulate drugs for targeted delivery. Microgels are crosslinked polymer networks that can swell in response to stimuli, releasing drugs in a controlled manner. Dendrimers are highly branched polymers with defined structures, allowing for precise drug loading and release. Hydrogels are water-swollen polymer networks that can release drugs in response to environmental changes. Other markets in polymer drug delivery include nanoparticles, liposomes, and conjugated polymers, each offering unique advantages for drug delivery applications.</p></p>
