Corrective Care aims to address specific issues or injuries, often involving a treatment plan to restore function over time. Relief Care provides immediate alleviation of pain or discomfort, offering short-term solutions. Each type serves distinct patient needs, promoting overall health and recovery through tailored chiropractic interventions.</p></p>
