<p><p>The passive fire protection coatings market is primarily divided into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions, easy application, and quicker drying times. They are often preferred for interior applications. Solvent-based coatings, on the other hand, use organic solvents and typically provide superior durability and resistance, making them suitable for exterior applications or harsher environments. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing fire resistance in various construction and industrial settings.</p></p>
