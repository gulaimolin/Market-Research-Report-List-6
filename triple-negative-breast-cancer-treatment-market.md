<p><p>The Triple-Negative Breast Cancer (TNBC) treatment market encompasses various therapeutic agents. Alkylating agents work by directly damaging DNA, impeding cancer cell replication. Microorganism products involve treatments sourced from microbes that may exhibit anti-cancer properties. Antimetabolites interfere with DNA and RNA synthesis, hindering cell division.
