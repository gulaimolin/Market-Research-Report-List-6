<p><p>Electrical digital twin software market types include System Twin, Process Twin, Product Twin, and Parts Twin. System Twin focuses on replicating the entire electrical system, from power generation to consumption. Process Twin simulates the operations and efficiency of electrical processes. Product Twin replicates individual electrical products, allowing for detailed analysis and optimization. Parts Twin focuses on specific components within the electrical system, assessing performance and functionality. Each type of digital twin serves a unique purpose in enhancing the understanding, monitoring, and management of electrical systems.</p></p>
