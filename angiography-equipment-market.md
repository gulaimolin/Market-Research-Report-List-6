<p><strong>The Angiography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Angiography</li><li>CT Angiography</li><li>MR Angiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angiography equipment market comprises three main types: X-Ray Angiography, CT Angiography, and MR Angiography. X-Ray Angiography utilizes X-ray imaging to visualize blood vessels after contrast injection, offering real-time imaging for cardiovascular assessments. CT Angiography employs computed tomography to produce detailed cross-sectional images, enhancing the visualization of vascular structures with high precision. MR Angiography uses magnetic resonance imaging to create detailed images of blood vessels without ionizing radiation, making it a safer alternative for vascular evaluation. Each type serves specific diagnostic needs.</p></p>
