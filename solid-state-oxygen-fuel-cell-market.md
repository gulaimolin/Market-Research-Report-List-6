<p><p>Solid State Oxygen Fuel Cell Market can be categorized into two main types: Planar and Tubular. Planar fuel cells are flat and composed of multiple layers stacked on top of each other, while tubular fuel cells have a cylindrical shape with electrolyte material running through the center. Planar fuel cells are more suited for portable applications due to their compact design, while tubular fuel cells are preferred for stationary power generation due to their higher efficiency and power output capabilities.</p></p>
