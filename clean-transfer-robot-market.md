<p><p>The Clean Transfer Robot market is categorized into two main types: Atmospheric Transfer Robots and Vacuum Transfer Robots. Atmospheric Transfer Robots operate in environments where air and particulate contamination is minimal, effectively handling sensitive materials without compromising cleanliness. In contrast, Vacuum Transfer Robots utilize vacuum technology to move items, ensuring contamination-free transfers in controlled environments, such as laboratories and cleanrooms. Both types are crucial for industries requiring high levels of hygiene and precision in material handling.</p></p>
