<p><p>The emboli removal catheter market is segmented into two main types: single lumen catheters and double lumen catheters. Single lumen catheters are designed with one channel for aspiration or retrieval of emboli, offering simplicity and ease of use. Both types are crucial in treating vascular blockages, aiming to improve patient outcomes in interventional procedures.</p></p>
