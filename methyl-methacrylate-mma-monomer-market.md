<p><p>The Methyl Methacrylate (MMA) monomer market includes several production methods: the ACH method, isobutylene method, and ethylene method. The ACH method utilizes acetone and hydrogen cyanide as precursors, while the isobutylene method involves the reaction of isobutylene with methacrylic acid. The ethylene method, on the other hand, derives MMA from ethylene via a series of chemical transformations. Each method varies in efficiency, cost, and environmental impact, influencing its adoption across various industrial applications.</p></p>
