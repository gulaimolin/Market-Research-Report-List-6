<p><p>The Fluorosilicone Elastomer (FVMQ) market is categorized into three types: liquid, solid, and semisolid FVMQ. Liquid FVMQ exhibits excellent fluid resistance and flexibility, making it suitable for applications in automotive and aerospace industries. Solid FVMQ offers superior mechanical strength and thermal stability, ideal for seals and gaskets. Semisolid FVMQ combines properties of both liquid and solid variants, providing a balance of flexibility and durability, often used in coatings and adhesives for demanding environments.</p></p>
