<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Operation</li><li>Air powered Operation</li><li>Electric Operation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cement grouting machine market comprises three main types based on operation: hydraulic, air-powered, and electric. Hydraulic operation machines use fluid pressure to inject cement slurry, offering high force and precision, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Air-powered machines utilize compressed air for pumping grout, providing portability and ease of use in various environments. Electric operation machines rely on electric motors, ensuring consistent power supply and reducing noise, making them suitable for indoor applications where emissions are a concern.</p></p>
