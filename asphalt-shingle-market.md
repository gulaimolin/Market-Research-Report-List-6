<p><ul><li>Glass Fibre</li><li>Carbon Fibre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt shingle market primarily features two types: glass fiber and organic (often referred to as carbon fiber). Glass fiber shingles are composed of a fiberglass mat coated with asphalt, providing enhanced durability, fire resistance, and lighter weight. They are popular for their longevity and eco-friendliness. In contrast, organic shingles use a mat made from recycled paper or felt, offering good insulation but greater susceptibility to moisture damage. Both types cater to differing preferences in durability and cost-effectiveness in roofing solutions.</p></p>
