<p><p>Medical solid waste disposal market types include incineration, landfill, autoclave, chemical treatment, and other methods. Autoclave treatment uses high-pressure steam to sterilize waste. Chemical treatment involves treating waste with disinfectants to kill pathogens. Each method has its own set of benefits and limitations in terms of effectiveness, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
