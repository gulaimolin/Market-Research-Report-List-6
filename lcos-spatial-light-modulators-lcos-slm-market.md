<p><p>LCoS Spatial Light Modulators (LCOS-SLM) are devices that manipulate light for various applications, including display technology and imaging systems. The market is primarily divided into two types: reflective LCOS-SLM and transmissive LCOS-SLM. Reflective LCOS-SLM reflects light off a micro-mirror array, making it suitable for high-contrast applications like projectors. In contrast, transmissive LCOS-SLM passes light through liquid crystal layers, allowing for high-resolution displays in devices like televisions. Both types cater to different industry needs, driving their distinct market demands.</p></p>
