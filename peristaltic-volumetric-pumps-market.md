<p><p>Peristaltic volumetric pumps are specialized devices used for moving fluids through flexible tubing. They operate by employing a rotor that compresses the tubing, creating a peristaltic movement that pushes the fluid forward while preventing cross-contamination. These pumps are widely used in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, wastewater treatment, and chemical processing, due to their precision and hygienic advantages.</p><p>The peristaltic volumetric pumps market is on a significant growth trajectory, driven by increasing demand for accurate fluid transfer in healthcare and industrial applications. In contrast, Frequency Speed Regulation utilizes electronic controls to adjust motor frequency, allowing for precise flow control and greater efficiency. Both technologies enhance the versatility and effectiveness of peristaltic pumps in various applications, such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and wastewater management.</p></p>
