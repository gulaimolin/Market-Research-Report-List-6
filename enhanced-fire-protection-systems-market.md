Active systems involve proactive measures such as sprinklers, fire alarms, and suppression systems that actively detect and extinguish fires. In contrast, passive systems focus on preventing the spread of fire and smoke through the use of fire-resistant materials, compartmentalization, and effective building design. Both systems work in tandem to enhance overall fire safety, addressing different aspects of fire risk and management.</p></p>
