<p><ul><li>Multiple Chanels</li><li>Single Chanel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary electrocardiographs market comprises two main types: single-channel and multiple-channel devices. Single-channel electrocardiographs provide basic heart rate and rhythm monitoring, making them suitable for small practices or quick assessments. In contrast, multiple-channel devices offer comprehensive monitoring by recording several leads simultaneously, enabling detailed analysis of heart conditions. This duality caters to varying clinical needs, with single-channel devices prized for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while multiple-channel units are favored for their advanced diagnostic capabilities in specialty practices.</p></p>
