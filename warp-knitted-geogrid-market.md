<p><strong>The Warp Knitted Geogrid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biaxial Tension</li><li>Uniaxial Tension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The warp knitted geogrid market is primarily categorized into biaxial and uniaxial tension types. Biaxial tension geogrids provide strength in two directions, making them suitable for applications requiring stability in both horizontal and vertical orientations, commonly used in road construction and soil reinforcement. Uniaxial tension geogrids, on the other hand, are designed to handle loads predominantly in one direction, providing enhanced strength for specific applications like slope stabilization and retaining structures. Both types enhance soil performance and structural integrity.</p></p>
