<p><ul><li>Air Pressured Hyperbaric Chamber</li><li>Oxygen Pressured Hyperbaric Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transport hyperbaric chamber market includes two primary types: air-pressured hyperbaric chambers and oxygen-pressured hyperbaric chambers. Air-pressured chambers use atmospheric air to create elevated pressure, assisting in treatments for conditions like decompression sickness. In contrast, oxygen-pressured chambers utilize pure oxygen at higher pressures, enhancing oxygen delivery to tissues and aiding in healing various medical conditions. Both types are integral in transport settings, providing timely hyperbaric therapy for patients in emergency or critical care situations.</p></p>
