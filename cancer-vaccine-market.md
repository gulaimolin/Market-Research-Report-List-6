<p><p>The cancer vaccine market comprises two main types: preventive and therapeutic vaccines. Preventive vaccines are designed to avert cancer development by stimulating the immune system against specific viruses or pathogens linked to cancer, such as HPV and hepatitis B. Conversely, therapeutic vaccines target existing cancer, aiming to enhance the immune response against tumor cells and improve patient outcomes. Both types play crucial roles in cancer management, offering innovative approaches to reduce incidence and improve survival rates.</p></p>
