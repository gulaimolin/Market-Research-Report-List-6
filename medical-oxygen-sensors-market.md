<p><p>The Medical Oxygen Sensors market primarily includes zirconia and optical oxygen sensors. Zirconia oxygen sensors utilize zirconium dioxide as a solid electrolyte, providing high accuracy and stability, making them ideal for critical medical applications. Optical oxygen sensors, on the other hand, employ fluorescence-based technology to measure oxygen levels, offering advantages such as rapid response times and non-invasive monitoring. Both sensor types play crucial roles in healthcare settings, ensuring precise oxygen measurement for patient safety and effective treatment.</p></p>
