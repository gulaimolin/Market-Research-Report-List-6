<p><p>The stainless steel flanges market comprises several key types, each serving distinct functions in piping systems. Weld flanges are designed for robust, permanent connections through welding, ensuring leak-proof joints. Blind flanges are utilized to seal the ends of piping, preventing flow and providing easy access for maintenance. Slip-on flanges offer a simple installation process by slipping over the pipe end, allowing for easy alignment. Other types include threaded and socket weld flanges, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
