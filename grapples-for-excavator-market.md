<p><p>The Grapples for Excavators market is primarily divided into two types: hydraulic and mechanical grapples. Hydraulic grapples utilize hydraulic power for enhanced gripping and maneuverability, making them suitable for handling heavy and awkward loads. They offer precision and speed, ideal for complex tasks. In contrast, mechanical grapples rely on a system of levers and cables, providing simplicity and lower maintenance costs. These are typically favored for lighter applications where cost-effectiveness and durability are prioritized.</p></p>
