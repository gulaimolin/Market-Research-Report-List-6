<p><p>The pleated membrane filter cartridge market includes various filter types, each suited for specific applications. PP filters, made of polypropylene, are cost-effective and resistant to chemicals. PES filters, constructed from polyethersulfone, offer high flow rates and thermal stability, ideal for biotechnology. PTFE filters, known for their hydrophobic properties, are excellent for air and gas filtration. Nylon filters provide strong chemical resistance and are often used for aqueous solutions. Each filter type caters to different industrial needs and performance specifications.</p></p>
