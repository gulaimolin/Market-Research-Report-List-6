<p><p>The commercial bain marie market is divided into two main types: wet heat and dry heat bain maries. Wet heat bain maries use water to maintain the temperature of food, providing consistent, gentle heating ideal for delicate dishes like sauces and custards. In contrast, dry heat bain maries operate without water, utilizing a heated surface to keep food warm, making them suitable for items like pastries or baked goods. Both types are essential for food service operations, ensuring food safety and quality.</p></p>
