<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Marine Desalination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RO</li><li>MSF</li><li>MED</li><li>ED/EDR/EDI</li><li>NF/SR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine desalination involves various technologies to convert seawater into potable water. Reverse Osmosis (RO) utilizes semi-permeable membranes to separate salt from water. Multi-Stage Flash (MSF) uses heat to evaporate and condense water in multiple stages. Multi-Effect Distillation (MED) employs multiple evaporators to enhance efficiency. Electrodialysis (ED), Electrodialysis Reversal (EDR), and Electrodialysis Ionization (EDI) use electric currents to drive ion transport, reducing salt content. Nanofiltration (NF) and Selective Reverse Osmosis (SR) focus on specific molecular separations, primarily for water purification.</p></p>
