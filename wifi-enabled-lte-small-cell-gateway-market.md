<p><p>The WiFi Enabled LTE Small Cell Gateway market comprises three key types: femtocells, microcells, and picocells. Femtocells are low-power cellular access points designed for residential use, enhancing indoor coverage. Microcells cover larger areas, typically for urban settings, improving service in dense environments. Picocells serve as intermediate solutions, bridging the gap between femtocells and microcells, often used in public venues like shopping malls. Together, these technologies enhance network capacity, coverage, and user experience in various environments.</p></p>
