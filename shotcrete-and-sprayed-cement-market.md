<p><p>Shotcrete and sprayed cement market can be categorized into two main types - wet spraying and dry spraying. In wet spraying, the mixture of cement and water is pre-mixed before being sprayed onto the surface, resulting in a more uniform application. On the other hand, dry spraying involves spraying dry cement mix onto the surface and then adding water to activate the cement. Both methods have their advantages and are commonly used in construction for reinforcing structures and creating durable surfaces.</p></p>
