<p><p>E-learning platforms can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access learning materials and resources from anywhere with an internet connection. This model offers scalability and reduced maintenance costs. In contrast, on-premise platforms are installed locally on a user's hardware, providing greater control over data and customization. However, they require significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, limiting accessibility compared to cloud-based options.</p></p>
