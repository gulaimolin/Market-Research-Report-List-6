<p><p>Organic banana powder is available in two main market types: freeze dried and spray dried. Freeze dried banana powder involves removing the moisture from the fruit using a low-temperature vacuum process, resulting in a more nutrient-dense powder with a longer shelf life. On the other hand, spray dried banana powder is made by spraying a liquid banana mixture into a hot air chamber, quickly drying it into a fine powder. Both types of banana powder offer convenience and versatility in various food and beverage applications.</p></p>
