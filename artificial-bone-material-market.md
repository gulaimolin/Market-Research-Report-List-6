<p><p>The artificial bone material market is categorized into three main types: inorganic, organic, and composite materials. Inorganic materials, often consisting of ceramics like hydroxyapatite, mimic natural bone structure and provide excellent biocompatibility. Organic materials, such as collagen-based products, promote cell adhesion and tissue integration. Composite materials combine inorganic and organic components to enhance mechanical strength and biological properties, offering a versatile solution for bone repair and regeneration. Each type caters to specific clinical needs in orthopedic and dental applications.</p></p>
