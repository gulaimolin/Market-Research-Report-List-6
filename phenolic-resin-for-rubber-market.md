<p><ul><li>Tackifying Resin</li><li>Reinforcing Resin</li><li>Adhesive Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phenolic resin for rubber encompasses three primary market types: tackifying resin, reinforcing resin, and adhesive resin. Tackifying resins enhance the adhesion and cohesion of rubber compounds, improving their grip and flexibility. Reinforcing resins increase the mechanical strength and durability of rubber products, providing enhanced performance in various applications. Adhesive resins are utilized to bond rubber to substrates, ensuring strong and reliable connections. Together, these resins play a crucial role in optimizing rubber formulations for diverse industrial uses.</p></p>
