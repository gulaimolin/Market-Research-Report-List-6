Atrioventricular Block involves impaired electrical conduction between the atria and ventricles, requiring pacing for effective heart rhythm. Sinus Node Dysfunction results in irregular heartbeats, necessitating pacing to restore normal function. Atrial Fibrillation, characterized by rapid heart rates, may also require pacing in certain cases. The "Others" segment includes various cardiac conditions where transcatheter pacing is applicable.</p></p>
