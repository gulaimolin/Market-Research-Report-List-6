<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive starter market can be categorized into three main types: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic systems. Electric starters are the most common, utilizing electrical energy to initiate engine cranking. Pneumatic starters use compressed air, providing a lightweight and efficient option, particularly for large engines. Hydraulic starters harness pressurized hydraulic fluid to generate power, offering high torque for heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct automotive needs, with electric starters dominating the passenger vehicle segment and pneumatic and hydraulic options preferred in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
