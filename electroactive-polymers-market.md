<p><p>The Electroactive Polymers market is primarily divided into two types: Electronic Type and Ionic Type Electroactive Polymers. Electronic Type Electroactive Polymers operate through the movement of charge carriers and are often used in applications like sensors and actuators due to their high conductivity and stability. In contrast, Ionic Type Electroactive Polymers rely on the movement of ions within the material, making them suitable for applications such as artificial muscles and soft robotics, where flexibility and high deformation are essential.</p></p>
