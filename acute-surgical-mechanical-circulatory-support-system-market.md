ECMO devices provide temporary cardiac and respiratory support by oxygenating blood outside the body, while EVADs assist the heart in pumping blood. Other devices in this market may include intra-aortic balloon pumps or temporary total artificial heart systems. These systems are utilized in acute settings to provide critical circulatory support in surgical and emergency situations.</p></p>
